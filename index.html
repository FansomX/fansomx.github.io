Fan Xiao

Hi! I'm Fan Xiao (肖帆). I'm currently a PhD candidate at LMU Adaptive Radiation Therapy Lab in Munich. My research interests are in the intersection between Deep Learning, Medical Imaging Processing and Monte Carlo Simulation. More specifically:

  • AI-accelerated dose calculation for photon and proton therapy
  • AI-accelerated prompt gamma emission prediction
  • AI-based air cavity segmentation on MRI

Prior to the PhD, I finished my Master's Degree in Biomedical Engineering at Southern Medical University in GuangZhou.

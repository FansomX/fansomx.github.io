About

PhD candidate in medical physics at LMU Munich building GPU-native dose engines for photon and proton therapy. Research blends differentiable Monte Carlo, real-time adaptive planning, and QA automation with clinical partners.

Research Focus

Differentiable Dose Engines

Monte Carlo surrogates and differentiable transport models tuned for MR-guided photon therapy, delivering QA-grade accuracy on GPUs.

Adaptive Radiotherapy Systems

GPU-first optimisation kernels that close the loop between deformable registration, plan adaptation, and online replanning in minutes.

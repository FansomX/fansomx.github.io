Fan Xiao

Hi! I'm Fan Xiao (肖帆). I'm currently a PhD candidate at LMU Adaptive Radiation Therapy Lab in Munich. My research focuses on deep learning for medical physics, specifically:

  • AI-accelerated dose calculation for photon and proton therapy
  • AI-accelerated prompt gamma emission prediction
  • AI-based air cavity segmentation on MRI

Prior to my PhD, I completed my Master's degree in Biomedical Engineering at Southern Medical University in Guangzhou.

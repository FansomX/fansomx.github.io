Fan Xiao

Hi! I'm Fan Xiao (肖帆⛵). I'm currently a PhD candidate at LMU Adaptive Radiation Therapy Lab in Munich. My research focuses on deep learning for medical imaging, specifically:

  • AI-accelerated Sequence Modeling for Dose Calculation
  • Photon/proton Monte Carlo Simulation
  • MRI/CT/CBCT segmentation/registration

Before my PhD, I obtained my Master's in Biomedical Engineering from Southern Medical University and my Bachelor's in Communication Engineering from Wuhan University of Technology.
